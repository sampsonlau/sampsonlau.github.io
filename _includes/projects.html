Projects

OpenLoop Alliance

OpenLoop is a multi-campus alliance competing in the SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competition. Our goal is to build a working pod prototype that will compete on a one-mile test track. The team at Cornell is designing the air bearing suspension system that the pod rides on.

I have developed physical models for the compression system and the air bearing suspension, and I am currently directing the battery pack development efforts.

Zero Ground Given

Zero Ground Given is an annual dance competition in Ithaca, NY created by the breakdance clubs of Cornell University and Ithaca College. The first edition of the event took place on May 1-2, 2015, bringing in some of the most skilled dancers in the Northeast.

I led the organization of the portion hosted at Cornell, which involves inviting judges, fundraising, logistics, and publicity.
